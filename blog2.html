Pumpkin Spice Latte

Oct 16th 2022 8 Min read

How we make our special pumpkin spice latte! Craving for pumpkin spice latte?

You can Easily make yours with all ingridents we use to make spice latte from our store. Now lets dive into the process of making Pumpkin Spice Latte!

Fall Favorite!!!!.Nutmeg, Cinnamon and Cloves — help bring all those happy thoughts and memories to mind.

1) Brewed Coffee: Brew 1/2 a cup of your favorite hot coffee, or make 2 espresso shots. Or use cold brew coffee and serve this latte cold, over ice.

2) Milk: Any kind of milk: whole milk, skim milk, coconut milk, cashew milk, oat milk, or almond milk. Read about the healthiest dairy options here.

3) Pumpkin Puree: Canned pumpkin puree is perfect; just make sure the only ingredient is pumpkin. You’ll only need a few tablespoons, so toss the rest in a smoothie or save for a future pumpkin spice latte (trust me, you’ll want to make this again and the puree will keep in the fridge for about a week).

4) Maple Syrup: Most of us are accustomed to the sweet taste of a Starbucks pumpkin spice latte. That’s no surprise since the OG (original) contains 50 grams of SUGAR! In this homemade latte, you’re going to use just a touch of maple syrup for a classic fall flavor.

5) Vanilla Extract: Just a touch for flavor. Go with the real deal; not imitation.

6) Pumpkin Pie Spice: Pumpkin puree is flavorless so the real flavor comes from this ingredient.
