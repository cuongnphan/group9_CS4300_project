Why Choose Us?

We are a small coffee shop located in Houston. We pride ourselves on the variety of different coffee beans we provide from different countries that allows customers to experience different cultures and flavors. You can explore the world right from our small shop. And, if you find your favorite coffee at our shop you can even buy the beans so you can make it at home. However, we can't promise it will taste as good as when we make it. :)
